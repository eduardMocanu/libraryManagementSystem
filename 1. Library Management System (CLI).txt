1. Library Management System (CLI)

    OOP focus: Model classes like Book, Member, Library, Loan with appropriate relationships (composition, aggregation).

    Data structures: Use collections (ArrayList for books and members, HashMap for quick book lookup by ISBN or member ID).

    I/O: Save/load book and member data from files (e.g., CSV or serialized objects).

    Features:

        Add/remove books and members

        Loan/return books

        Search books by title, author, or ISBN

        List overdue loans (based on due date)

    Bonus: Implement sorting of books by title or author.
